Enter a BIP39 mnemonic to deterministically derive a universe_id, then open a portal (e.g. the Automaton Bundle tab) at ?base=.

Notes:
  • This validates BIP39 checksum against the English wordlist (loaded locally from bip39_english.txt).
  • Universe derivation is sha256(entropy) (first 16 hex chars), so it’s deterministic from the mnemonic.
